## Overview:
Simple batch script for rclone with a convenient menu and the ability to configure through the configuration file.

![screenshot](https://github.com/Unix-MTv/rclone-sync/blob/master/rclone-sync.png)

## How-to:
##### rclone-sync/
##### ├── app
##### │   ├── cecho.exe
##### │   └── rclone.exe
##### ├── conf<br />
##### │   ├── exclude-from.txt
##### │   ├── rclone.conf
##### │   └── rclone-sync.conf
##### ├── log
##### │   └── log.txt
##### ├── rclone-sync.bat
##### └── sync
